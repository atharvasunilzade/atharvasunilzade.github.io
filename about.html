About Us

The workshop behind your software

BlackForgeX Pvt. Ltd. is a software development company based in Akola, India. We build, ship and support custom software and tailored solutions for businesses that want technology shaped around them.

Our mission

Great software shouldn't be a luxury. We make custom, reliable, well-supported software accessible to businesses of every size — engineered with craft and backed for the long run.

Like a forge turns raw metal into precise tools, we turn ideas and requirements into dependable software that does real work. We care about how it's built, how it runs, and how it holds up over time.

Craft

Clean, maintainable code we're proud to put our name on.

Partnership

We work with you, not just for you — through launch and beyond.

Reliability

Software that's secure, stable and supported, not abandoned.

Clarity

Honest timelines, plain language, transparent progress.
